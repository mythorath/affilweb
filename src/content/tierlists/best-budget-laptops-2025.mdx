## What to Look for in a Budget Laptop

When shopping for an affordable laptop, prioritize these key features:

### Performance
- **AMD Ryzen 5** or **Intel Core i5** processors offer the best value
- **8GB RAM** minimum for smooth multitasking
- **SSD storage** for faster boot times and responsiveness

### Build Quality
- **Solid hinges** that won't break after a year
- **Decent keyboard** for comfortable typing
- **Port selection** including USB-C and HDMI

### Display & Battery
- **1080p resolution** minimum for clear text and images
- **6+ hour battery life** for all-day productivity
- **Anti-glare coating** reduces eye strain

## Budget Laptop Categories

### For Students ($300-500)
Perfect for note-taking, research, and basic productivity. Look for good battery life and lightweight design.

### For Work ($500-700)
Better performance for professional tasks. Video calls, spreadsheets, and multitasking should run smoothly.

### Light Gaming ($600-800)
Entry-level gaming with integrated graphics. Can handle older games and esports titles at lower settings.

## Money-Saving Tips

1. **Shop during sales** - Back-to-school and Black Friday offer best deals
2. **Consider refurbished** - Business-grade laptops often available at great prices
3. **Check specifications** - Don't just look at the processor, RAM and storage matter too
4. **Read reviews** - Real user experiences reveal long-term reliability issues

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is 4GB of RAM enough for a budget laptop?**
A: We recommend 8GB minimum. 4GB will feel sluggish with modern websites and applications.

**Q: Should I buy a Chromebook instead?**
A: Chromebooks are great for basic tasks and have excellent battery life, but Windows laptops offer more software compatibility.

**Q: How long should a budget laptop last?**
A: With proper care, a good budget laptop should serve you well for 3-4 years of regular use.
